# Constraints & Style Requirements

## Deck Style
- Template: templates/company/base-template.pptx
- Slide ratio: 16:9
- Background: White
- Color tone: Company-approved blue tones only

## Typography (Mandatory)
- Title: MS고딕네오 Bold 24pt
- Governing message: MS고딕네오 Bold 16pt
- Body: MS고딕네오 Regular 12pt (default), max 14pt if needed
- Footnote: MS고딕네오 10pt

## Density
- Bullets per slide: 3–6
- Prefer 1-line bullets; max 2 lines per bullet
- Avoid paragraphs; avoid “wall of text”

## Content Tone
- Executive, factual, structured (MECE)
- Clearly separate facts vs assumptions
- Use actionable implications, not generic descriptions

## Deliverable Packaging
- PPTX output path: clients/<client>/outputs/
- Keep research notes and sources in clients/<client>/research/ and sources.md
